Resume

Director of Engineering, 2020 - present

  • Developed org chart, hiring plan, and philosophies for 30+ person Enterprise Services teams throughout the United States and Canada, then built the teams through hiring and development.
  • Led initiatives to integrate technology teams and technology applications during an acquisition. This initiative brought consumer functionality into our applications and my teams were integral in the back end infrastructure integrations during the acquisition.
  • Served as management lead for all Internal Audit, Compliance, and Privacy initiatives, including prioritizing and ensuring deliverables by the tech teams to meet timelines for compliance and risk remediation.
  • Mentored and trained multiple team members to become servant-driven team leaders. Led and mentored through the Aspiring Leaders Program, the Experience IT Detroit Program, and the Leader 2 Leader Program.
  • Enterprise Infrastructure – directly led the cloud infrastructure team supporting our AWS stack running client facing applications. Over two years, implemented cloud-first solutions including GitHub Actions, Terraform Cloud, and AWS ECS to retire legacy CI/CD Jenkins server and Elastic Beanstalk implementation.
  • Information Security – built a mature security practice managing and securing our web applications with a focus on activity monitoring and incident response, including deploying SDLC standards and policies for the entire organization. Along the way, implemented SAST and DAST tools and testing into the deployment cycle.
  • Enterprise Service Bus – hired and built the team responsible for architecting and implementing an enterprise service bus for Rocket Homes built in AWS using Kafka and Mulesoft.
  • NOC & Uptime - Led teams responsible for monitoring and triaging any uptime impact on our applications, including third-party contracted and direct hire teams. Led process initiatives to ensure runbooks, response times, and SLAs are met by all engineering teams in the company.
